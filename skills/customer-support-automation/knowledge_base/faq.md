# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Authentication

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to the login page → click "Forgot Password" → enter your email → check your inbox for the reset link. The link expires in 24 hours.

**Q: I'm locked out of my account**
A: Try clearing your browser cookies and cache. If still locked out, contact support with your registered email and we'll help within 2 hours.

**Q: How do I enable 2FA?**
A: Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication → follow the setup wizard. Supports Google Authenticator and SMS.

---

### Billing & Subscription

**Q: How do I upgrade my plan?**
A: Settings → Billing → Upgrade Plan → select your new tier → confirm payment. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. Submit a refund request via this form [link] or email billing@example.com with your account email.

**Q: Where can I find my invoices?**
A: Settings → Billing → Invoice History → download PDFs. Invoices are also emailed automatically on each billing cycle.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription → follow the steps. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is loading slowly**
A: Try: (1) Clear browser cache, (2) Check your internet connection, (3) Disable browser extensions, (4) Try a different browser. If the issue persists, submit a bug report.

**Q: I'm getting a 500 error**
A: 500 errors are usually temporary. Wait 2-3 minutes and retry. If it persists > 10 minutes, check our status page at [status.example.com] and contact support if the status shows green.

**Q: Data not syncing**
A: (1) Force refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R), (2) Check if you're on a stable internet connection, (3) Log out and back in. Still not working? Submit a bug report with your browser/OS details.

---

### Integrations

**Q: How do I connect to Slack?**
A: Settings → Integrations → Slack → Authorize → select channel. See full guide at [docs.example.com/slack].

**Q: Can I use the API with my plan?**
A: API access is available on Pro and Enterprise plans. Generate your API key at Settings → Developer → API Keys.

---

### Data & Privacy

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Settings → Privacy → Export Data → choose format (CSV/JSON) → confirm. Export typically completes within 30 minutes and you'll receive an email when ready.

**Q: Do you comply with GDPR?**
A: Yes. We are fully GDPR-compliant. See our Privacy Policy at [example.com/privacy] and request data deletion via Settings → Privacy → Delete My Data.
